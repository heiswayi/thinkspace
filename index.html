Research interest

I am broadly interested in distributed systems. I believe theory and systems go hand in hand.
Currently, I am building a machine learning pipeline that will eventually be run on disaggregated storage. The goal is to pressure storage and pin point the bottlenecks via a benchmark. This work is being done with my advisors Prof. Ada Gavrilovska and Prof. Kexin Rong .
Apart from above, I am also working with Prof. Joy Arulraj to make query execution faster on videos by adding probabilitic predicates which are light weight and Prof. Alexey Tumanov to create a compeletely elastic deep learning training platform. Feel free to contact me to discuss any one of the above or something else that I can help with or contribute to.
